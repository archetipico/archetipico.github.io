Biography

Since 1997, I've called Milan my home, cherishing its unique culture and dynamic changes over the years. I attended a Scientific Lyceum, where I had the opportunity to present a thesis on the intersection of malwares and art.

Now, as I approach the completion of my studies in Computer Science from Universita' degli Studi di Milano, drafting a thesis that focuses on developing a visual language tailored for the semantic annotation of web tables.

In my professional journey, I've dedicated myself, since 2017, to my role as a Systems Analyst, where I had the opportunity to contribute to both the production and testing of a cloud-based software designed for document management.

I have a keen interest in Information Security and Scientific Visualization. In my free time, I enjoy studying Computer Graphics and exploring various other hobbies.
