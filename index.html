Principles

Against unwanted profiling: user profiling not only leads to security concerns, but it also creates inequalities and biased standards.

Against excess of advertising: advertisements bombing leaves people in "bubbles" where they will only see what the provider thinks they want to see.

Against excess of social media: social media addiction deprives people from energy, time and focus and it leads to depression or social anxiety.

Against mass surveillance: an unregulated use may lead to discrimination or to a higer rate of cybersecurity attacks.

Against pollution: you can start by saving electricity and hardware space, like this website 😉

In favour of open source software: no, you don't need a closed source messaging app, everyone knows how it's made. No, open source doesn't mean free. And no, closed source doesn't give you more security, check Kerckhoffs's principle.
