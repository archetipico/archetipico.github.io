Welcome!

The guy in the picture up there is me, and this is my website 😎
I was born in 1997 in Milan and since the age of 3 I wanted to learn about computers:
I remember me playing videogames like SimCity 3000 without knowing English and without
understanding what I had to do, but I've always been a curious boy, and the thought of
"science things" like wires and electricity, mixed togheter, to create some beautiful ouput
always got me wondering how could that be possible.

When I was 13 years old I wanted to learn programming by myself, so I obviously picked a
very simple and high level language for beginners, like C++, and I became what is known as a script
kiddie. Of course, without knowledge, a mentor, books,... I ended up with a calculator.cpp
and still without the understanding of what's happening inside my computer.
One year later I will enroll in a Scientific Lyceum, hoping to find my answer there.

Once I graduated from school, in the year 2016, I immediatly decided to enroll in a
Computer Science university degree. The best choice of my life. There I learnt
how to really understand machines, how to do math, how to solve problems, how to program and
how to see the world with different eyes.

A year later I also started an apprenticeship as a Systems Analyst, that
after 3 years converted in a real job I still am busy with. I help my company in choosing
technologies, testing and programming, for clients or for our business itself.
